TIL that:

  1. <html> is the ancestor - every other tag is its child.
  2. <h1> is the child of <body>, but not indented.
  3. <span> is the child of <h1> and the descendant of <body>.
  4. "&" + "lt" = "<", "&" + "gt" = ">".
  5. <p> and <h1> are siblings. (not nested)
