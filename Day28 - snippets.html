TIL that:

  1. Users can create their own new snippets by clicking Tools > Developers > New Snippet.
  2. ${n:this} shows where the exact nth tap on Tab will lead you to.
  3. margin: ${1:0} ${2:0} ${3:0} ${4:0};
  4. It's important to rename your custom snippet with the extension name "sublime-snippet", otherwise it won't work...
